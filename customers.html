Customers

If you want to reach the population of West Africa, one channel stands out: the mobile phone.

Virtually every adult in the region has a phone. According to the World Bank, the Gambia's mobile subscription rate is 111 percent. Senegal's is 114 percent. Sierra Leone's is 86 per cent.

Alchemy has connections into all of the mobile networks that serve these mobile users. Our products help public and private enterprises to…

  • Send SMS messages in bulk
  • Send rich messages in bulk (WhatsApp, iMessage etc)
  • Acquire your own short codes
  • Send one-time security passcodes
  • Make cloud-based voice calls
  • Use APIs to automate the sending of messages and calls

We work with three types of customers…

Enterprises

Want to communicate with your customers personally, and in real time? Want to give them a channel through which to reply?

It's time to look beyond email, billboards, TV or radio.

Alchemy will help you open up the mobile channel. It's easy to get started. You don't need specialist training, and you can control your costs with fixed monthly fees. You can use our services to send messages such as:

  • "You just paid 20 dalasi"
  • "Here is your daily horoscope"

Government

Governments in West Africa face two key challenges when communicating with citizens: ensuring they reach the entire adult population and verifying the identity of people who may lack official documents (or an address).

But (almost) everyone has a phone. This makes the mobile an excellent channel through which to send alerts – and also a reliable source of identity.

This is why more and more government departments use Alchemy services to send important notices such as:

  • "Your appointment is tomorrow at 9.30am"
  • "It is safe to fish today"
  • "Use this code to register"

Alchemy's government customers include:

  • Department Of Fisheries
  • Gambia Revenue Authority
  • Ministry Of Health
  • Gambia Fire and Rescue Services
